The '''2016–17 UEFA Champions League group stage''' began on 13 September and ended on 7 December 2016. A total of 32 teams competed in the group stage to decide the 16 places in the [[2016–17 UEFA Champions League knockout phase|knockout phase]] of the [[2016–17 UEFA Champions League]].<ref name="regulations">{{cite web |title=Regulations of the UEFA Champions League 2016/17 Season |url=http://www.uefa.org/MultimediaFiles/Download/Regulations/uefaorg/Regulations/02/35/87/89/2358789_DOWNLOAD.pdf |website=UEFA.com |publisher=Union of European Football Associations |accessdate=13 June 2016 }}</ref>

==Draw==
The draw was held on 25 August 2016, 18:00 [[Central European Summer Time|CEST]], at the [[Grimaldi Forum]] in [[Monaco]].<ref>{{cite web |title=UEFA Champions League group stage draw |url=http://www.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/season=2017/draws/round=2000783/index.html |website=UEFA.com |publisher=Union of European Football Associations |accessdate=13 June 2016 }}</ref> The 32 teams were drawn into eight groups of four, with the restriction that teams from the same association could not be drawn against each other. For the draw, the teams were seeded into four pots based on the following principles:<ref name="pot1">{{cite web |title=Madrid, Leicester among Champions League seeds |url=http://www.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/news/newsid=2359976.html |website=UEFA.com |publisher=Union of European Football Associations |date=28 May 2016 |accessdate=13 June 2016 }}</ref><ref name="regulations"/>
*Pot 1 contained the title holders and the champions of the top seven associations based on their 2015 [[UEFA coefficient#Country coefficient|UEFA country coefficients]].<ref name="country coefficients">{{cite web |title=Country coefficients 2014/15 |url=http://www.uefa.com/memberassociations/uefarankings/country/season=2015/index.html |website=UEFA.com |publisher=Union of European Football Associations |accessdate=13 June 2016 }}</ref><ref name="crank">{{cite web |title=UEFA Country Ranking 2015 |url=http://kassiesa.home.xs4all.nl/bert/uefa/data/method4/crank2015.html |website=kassiesa.home.xs4all.nl |publisher=Bert Kassies |accessdate=13 June 2016}}</ref>
*Pots 2, 3 and 4 contained the remaining teams, seeded based on their 2016 [[UEFA coefficient#Club coefficient|UEFA club coefficients]].<ref name="club coefficients">{{cite web |title=Club coefficients 2015/16 |url=http://www.uefa.com/memberassociations/uefarankings/club/season=2016/index.html |website=UEFA.com |publisher=Union of European Football Associations |accessdate=13 June 2016 }}</ref><ref name="trank">{{cite web |title=UEFA Team Ranking 2016 |url=http://kassiesa.home.xs4all.nl/bert/uefa/data/method4/trank2016.html |website=kassiesa.home.xs4all.nl |publisher=Bert Kassies |accessdate=13 June 2016 }}</ref><ref name="seed">{{cite web |title=Seeding in the Champions League 2016/2017 |url=http://kassiesa.home.xs4all.nl/bert/uefa/seedcl2016.html |website=kassiesa.home.xs4all.nl |publisher=Bert Kassies |accessdate=13 June 2016 }}</ref>

Moreover, the draw was controlled for teams from the same association in order to split the teams evenly into the two sets of groups (A–D, E–H) for maximum television coverage.

The fixtures were decided after the draw. On each matchday, four groups play their matches on Tuesday, while the other four groups play their matches on Wednesday, with the two sets of groups (A–D, E–H) alternating between each matchday. There are other restrictions: for example, teams from the same city (e.g., [[Real Madrid C.F.|Real Madrid]] and [[Atlético Madrid]]) in general do not play at home on the same matchday (UEFA tries to avoid teams from the same city playing at home on the same day or on consecutive days, due to logistics and crowd control), and teams in certain countries (e.g., Belarus, Russia, Kazakhstan) do not play at home on the last matchday (due to cold weather and simultaneous kick-off times).

On 17 July 2014, the UEFA emergency panel ruled that Ukrainian and Russian clubs would not be drawn against each other "until further notice" due to the [[2014 pro-Russian unrest in Ukraine|political unrest]] between the countries.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.uefa.org/about-uefa/news/newsid=2124836.html|title=Emergency Panel decisions|publisher=UEFA.org|date=17 July 2014}}</ref> Therefore, Ukrainian club [[FC Dynamo Kyiv|Dynamo Kyiv]] (Pot 3) and Russian clubs [[PFC CSKA Moscow|CSKA Moscow]] (Pot 1) and [[FC Rostov|Rostov]] (Pot 4) could not be drawn into the same group.

==Format==
In each group, teams play against each other home-and-away in a [[round-robin tournament|round-robin format]]. The group winners and runners-up advance to the [[2016–17 UEFA Champions League knockout phase|round of 16]], while the third-placed teams enter the [[2016–17 UEFA Europa League knockout phase|Europa League round of 32]].<ref name="regulations"/>

===Tiebreakers===
The teams are ranked according to [[Points (association football)|points]] (3 points for a win, 1 point for a draw, 0 points for a loss). If two or more teams are equal on points on completion of the group matches, the following criteria are applied in the order given to determine the rankings (regulations Article 17.01):<ref name="regulations"/>
#higher number of points obtained in the group matches played among the teams in question;
#superior [[goal difference]] from the group matches played among the teams in question;
#higher number of goals scored in the group matches played among the teams in question;
#higher number of [[away goals rule|goals scored away from home]] in the group matches played among the teams in question;
#if, after having applied criteria 1 to 4, teams still have an equal ranking, criteria 1 to 4 are reapplied exclusively to the matches between the teams in question to determine their final rankings. If this procedure does not lead to a decision, criteria 6 to 12 apply;
#superior goal difference in all group matches;
#higher number of goals scored in all group matches;
#higher number of away goals scored in all group matches;
#higher number of wins in all group matches;
#higher number of away wins in all group matches;
#lower disciplinary points total based only on yellow and red cards received in all group matches (red card = 3 points, yellow card = 1 point, expulsion for two yellow cards in one match = 3 points);
#higher [[UEFA coefficient#Club coefficient|club coefficient]].

==Groups==
The matchdays are 13–14 September, 27–28 September, 18–19 October, 1–2 November, 22–23 November, and 6–7 December 2016.<ref>{{cite web |title=2016/17 Champions League match calendar |url=http://www.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/season=2017/competitionformat/index.html |website=UEFA.com |publisher=Union of European Football Associations |accessdate=13 June 2016 }}</ref> The match kickoff times are 20:45 CEST/CET, except for matchday 5 in [[Russia]] which are 18:00 CET.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.uefa.com/MultimediaFiles/Download/competitions/General/02/39/91/09/2399109_DOWNLOAD.pdf|title=Full Fixture List|publisher=UEFA.com}}</ref> The match kickoff times for matchdays 4 and 5 in [[Turkey]] were changed from 20:45 CET to 18:45 CET due to the decision of the Turkish government to use the [[UTC+03:00|UTC+3]] time zone all year round starting from September 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/news/newsid=2407446.html|title=Changes to Champions League and Europa League kick-off times|publisher=UEFA.com|date=26 September 2016}}</ref>

Times up to 29 October 2016 (matchdays 1–3) are [[Central European Summer Time|CEST]] ([[UTC+02:00|UTC+2]]), thereafter (matchdays 4–6) times are [[Central European Time|CET]] ([[UTC+01:00|UTC+1]]).
